Setup

Required Materials

This version is meant to be played in-person, not online; so grab a couple of friends!
You'll want three devices to play. One for each Spymaster and one to function as the public board.
Typically, the Spymasters will use smartphones and you'll use something larger to be the public board.
Navigate to this webpage on all three devices.

Seed Synchronization

You'll want to make sure that everyone is playing the same game.
Check the value of the Seed below, and make sure it's the same on all three devices.
If they aren't, everyone should agree on a Seed to use and type it into the Seed box below and hit Enter.

How to Play

Some things are a little different in this digital version.
Clicking on a tile will reveal whether it belonged to Red Team, Blue Team, or was a Civilian or the Assassin.
So, try to refrain from unnecessary clicking or touching of the screen as you could accidentally reveal a spy.

If you are a Spymaster, click the appropriate button below.
Spymasters will see a button that reveals the entire board. Just like the 'Key' card in the board game!
Unlike the physical game, Spymasters don't have to sit next to each other. Just as long as nobody else can see your phone.
